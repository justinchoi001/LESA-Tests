LESA - Customer Feedback - P1 Escalation

Prerequisites:

  • Customer account
  • Liferay CSE account
  • Partner (non manager) account

Description:

This test is designed to have users test the feedback feature's pagination controls. Liferay CSE can view customer feedback which number in the hundreds and thousands. Pagination can be lost by advancing to the next page. Sign in as a CSE and assert that the pagination control preferences are not lost.

Road Map:

  1. Sign In as a CSE
  2. View Waiting Feedback
  3. Change Waiting Pagination Delta
  4. View All Feedback
  5. Change All Feedback Pagination Delta

Test Steps:

  1. Navigate to http://www-uat.liferay.com
  2. Click Menu > Sign In
  3. Enter the following:
    1. Email Address: ${liferay engineer email account}.broken
    2. Password: ${password}
  4. Click Sign In
  5. Click Menu > User Profile
  6. Click Support
  7. Click the Feedback tab
    View Waiting Feedback
  8. Click Waiting
  9. Assert the default number of Items is 20
    Change Waiting Pagination Delta
  10. Select 50 from the Item drop down
  11. Assert that 50 results display in the results area
  12. Click Next
  13. Assert that 50 results display in the results area
  14. Click Last
  15. Assert the number of Items is 50
  16. Click Previous
  17. Assert that 50 results display in the results area
  18. Click First
  19. Assert that 50 results display in the results area
    View All Feedback
  20. Click All Feedback
  21. Assert the default number of Items is 20
    Change All Pagination Delta
  22. Select 50 from the Item drop down
  23. Assert that 50 results display in the results area
  24. Click Next
  25. Assert that 50 results display in the results area
  26. Click Last
  27. Assert the number of Items is 50
  28. Click Previous
